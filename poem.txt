In night's embrace, we find our way,
To endless skies that greet the day.
Through valley deep, where shadows hide,
And every dream is pushed aside.
The rays of hope will light our path,

A glow that shines through storm and wrath.
With open hearts, we face the truth,
A never-ending bond, our proof.
The night may fall, but still we stand,
A always strong, hand in hand.

Through grief and loss, we’ll never bend,
Our infinite love will never end.
We vow to hold, to trust, to fight,
With every step, we climb new heights.

For you and I, we’ll make our mark,
In open skies, through light and dark,
Our understanding will create a spark. 

No undying fear will break our bond,
Our promise lives, forever fond.
